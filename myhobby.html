My Love for Dance

More about me!

Dancing is my true passion! I have been dancing for 7 years and I have been loving it ever since! My favorite styles of dance are hip-hop, lock, tut, house, and crump! One day, I really want to try ballet, contemporary, or tap. I got into dance through my cousin. He taught classes and just learning from him truly made me want to get better and better.

I love expressing myself through dance, and it's a way for me to stay connected to my creative energy. I feel the most confident when I am moving to the music and creating choreographies with rhythm and beats. Whether performing on stage or just freestyling at home, dancing always makes me feel alive and free!

If you want to check out my dance team, please visit TNT-K Official for dance covers, events, and updates!
